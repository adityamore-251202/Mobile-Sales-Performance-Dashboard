# Mobile Sales Performance Executive Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing regional mobile sales data to uncover key performance trends, consumer purchasing behaviors, and revenue distributions. Using data visualization best practices, I built an interactive executive dashboard designed to help stakeholders track sales KPIs, monitor brand performance, and identify growth opportunities across different geographic regions.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Business Impact
* **Revenue Breakdown:** Generated a total revenue of **769.20M** across **19K total units sold** over 4,000 transactions.
* **Payment Preferences:** Payment methods are exceptionally well-distributed, with UPI, Debit Cards, Credit Cards, and Cash each capturing roughly **24% to 26%** of total revenue. This indicates strong digital adoption alongside traditional methods.
* **Geographic Distribution:** Identified high-performing regional clusters across major cities in India, helping optimize supply chain and regional marketing efforts.
* **Customer Satisfaction:** Monitored customer sentiment across top brands (Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Vivo, Xiaomi), maintaining a steady average rating of **3.69**.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
* **Data Source:** `Mobile Sales Data.xlsx`
* **Dashboard Design:** Designed a dark-themed, high-contrast UI focused on executive scannability.
* **Data Skills:** Data cleaning, KPI metrics calculation, trend analysis, and geographic mapping.
